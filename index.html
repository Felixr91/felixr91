About Me

A New York native, Felix has a Bachelor's degreee in Audio and Video Production from SUNY New Paltz, as well as a Master's Degreee in Adolescent Special Education from CUNY Hunter College.

Starting with several self-taught courses on web programming, Felix went on to pursue web development more seriously while living in Boise Idaho.

Felix's passions have evolved thoughout the years but he remains passionate about teaching. Felix is interested in building tools and applications that help educators.

"Holla!" Kanban Boardz!

The "Holla!" kanban project was collaboratively built with friend and colleague, Malik Whitfield. The application utilizes Vue.js on the client side and Node.js on the server side. The concept of a kanban board was created by Taiichi Ohno, and is a work management tool that allows users to organize, set, and prioritize tasks. In Japanese, the word "Kan" means "visual" and "ban" means "card," so Kanban refers to visual cards. Click the link to register and test the app!
